Coach Information

My name is Charlemage Marc and I am a coach for SAC (Soccer Association of Columbia) . I have earned my USSF National E license. I am a product of SAC/HC's youth soccer Select program. With addition to playing for SAC, I played for Mount Saint Joseph High School. My goal is to pass my knowledge I learned playing down to this team. Select coaches are expected to attend and continue education courses and must apply each year to continue coaching at the Select level. We look forward to continuing to work with our players to teach skills, tactics, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

Select Information

This program is for more advanced players who demonstrate a higher skill level in technical (passing, dribbling, shooting) and tactical skills (aggressiveness, field vision, positioning, and communication). Our team will have two practices a week and players should attend all practices. In addition to the Fall and Spring seasons , we will likely play an indoor season. All of the players on the team will play at least 50 percent of each game. But, there will be some players who play more than others. This will be based on individual skill level, game conditions, player attitude, practice attendance, and the ability to play in the positions we require.
