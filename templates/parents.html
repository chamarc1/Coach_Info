Expectations of Parents

• Be positive with your son, the other players on the team, parents, coaches, the referees, and the teams we play. It will make it more fun for everyone.
• Please get your son to practice and games on time.
• If your son is going to miss a practice or a game, let the coaches know in advance.
• Referees will make mistakes – sometimes bad mistakes. It happens. We expect parents to take these in stride. SAC does not tolerate referee abuse, and as coaches we fully support this policy.
• Finally, if you have a question or issue with the way Umaru and I coach, please bring it to us directly. We want to avoid having a player who won’t listen because his parents disagree with the coaching and complain about the coaches in front of their son. Your son will pick up on this and will be less likely to feel a part of the team. This serves no real purpose and can be very disruptive.
