Expectations of Players

  1. Play hard & be prepared.
  2. Be at practice on time and ready to work hard. Bring a serious attitude to practice. We will have fun, but a player who is fooling around takes away from the team.
  3. When on the sideline during a game, players should support their teammates. Players should not “beg” to get in the game or to play a particular position.
  4. Be a good teammate. Everyone will make mistakes during the season – it happens. Encourage teammates; do not give them a hard time if they make an error.
  5. Finally, always, always, always demonstrate GOOD SPORTSMANSHIP.
